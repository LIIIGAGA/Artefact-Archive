I am a Digital Architect and Worldbuilder working at the intersection of architecture, game design, and interactive media.

My practice focuses on architectural storytelling, narrative environments, and immersive experiences. Using Unreal Engine, 3D production workflows, and narrative design methodologies, I create spaces that function not only as environments but as systems for storytelling and player engagement.

At The Bartlett's Cinematic & Videogame Architecture programme, I have developed projects across video games, immersive animation, film, and spatial audio. Across these disciplines, I am interested in how architecture can shape perception and player agency, and how spaces can become responsive narrative systems rather than passive backdrops.

Currently completing my MArch degree at The Bartlett, UCL, I am open to opportunities in game design, narrative environment design, worldbuilding, immersive experiences, and interactive media.

我是一名数字建筑师与世界构建者，在建筑、游戏设计与互动媒体的交叉领域从事创作。

我的实践聚焦于建筑叙事、叙事性环境与沉浸式体验。借助虚幻引擎、3D制作流程与叙事设计方法论，我创造的空间不仅是环境本身，更是叙事系统与玩家互动的载体。

在巴特莱特的电影与游戏建筑学项目中，我跨越游戏、沉浸式动画、影像与空间音频等多个领域进行创作。我持续探索建筑如何塑造感知与玩家自主性，以及空间如何成为响应式叙事系统，而非被动的背景。

目前正在完成巴特莱特建筑学院的建筑学硕士学位，欢迎游戏设计、叙事环境设计、世界构建、沉浸式体验及互动媒体等方向的合作机会。
