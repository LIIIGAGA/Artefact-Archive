// Overview

An experimental animated short film combining 2D collage and 3D animation, inspired by Rem Koolhaas' architectural manifesto Exodus.

Inspired by the Berlin Wall, Exodus imagines a linear architectural structure — the Strip — dividing London into two worlds: a decaying city beyond the walls and an idealized utopia within. The film reinterprets this vision as a critique of society's obsession with order, rationality, and the dream of the perfect city.

In the story, individuals entering the Strip are transformed into identical cubes that collectively form its monumental walls. As suppressed desires resurface, the rigid system begins to collapse. The cubes awaken, rebel, and turn the Strip into a playground of disorder, raising questions about freedom, control, and the possibility of escaping one system only to enter another.

以雷姆·库哈斯的建筑宣言《出埃及记》为灵感来源，结合2D拼贴和3D动画制作的实验动画短片。

库哈斯受到柏林墙的启发，想象了一个由高墙构成的线性建筑——"the Strip"，将伦敦切割为两半。墙外是被资本侵蚀逐渐没落的旧城，而墙内是被新秩序所构建的理想家园。从我的视角来看，这个乌托邦的构想实际上是在讽刺人们对于完美城市的梦想以及对理性和秩序的病态痴迷。

影片将这种讽喻通过叙事设计进一步增强。每一个自由的个体进入条带之后便会被转化为一模一样的方块。那些沉默的高墙便是由失去自由意志的方块组成。然而被压抑的欲望终将爆发，墙外的混乱向墙内渗透，觉醒的方块暴动，条带变成了游乐场。在最后的幻象中，方块们似乎打破了高墙的束缚，重获世界。但那是否是另一个自由的陷阱，我们不得而知。
