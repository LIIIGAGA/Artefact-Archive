// Overview

BubbleNight is a first-person immersive exploration game built in Unreal Engine 5, using the visual languages of Dreamcore and Poolrooms to reassemble fragments of childhood memory into a series of surreal liminal spaces.

The work centers on environmental storytelling — neon pink bleeding through damp tiled corridors, coloured orbs stacked quietly along winding slides, crystal roses blooming in silence across empty rooms. Each space is carefully composed through the interplay of light, material, and object, building an atmospheric tension that sits between comfort and unease, familiarity and estrangement.

《浮梦夜》是一款基于 Unreal Engine 5 开发的第一人称沉浸式场景探索游戏，以梦核（Dreamcore）与池核（Poolrooms）美学为核心视觉语言，将童年记忆的碎片重新编排为一组超现实的阈限空间。

项目的核心在于环境叙事——粉紫色霓虹渗透进潮湿的瓷砖走廊，彩色球体沿弯曲的滑道静静堆叠，水晶玫瑰在空旷的房间里无声盛开。每一个空间都经过细致的氛围营造，光、材质与物件的组合共同构成一种介于安全与不安、熟悉与疏离之间的情绪张力。
